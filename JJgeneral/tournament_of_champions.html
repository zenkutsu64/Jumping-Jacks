Great Parties at Jumping Jacks just got better!

Our experienced Party Coordinators will organize your party into teams, then supervise and coordinate a bunch of of super-fun games on our jumps! We'll keep score, give out prizes, and keep records of the best performances and post them so you can see how you compare!!

Any party can easily be turned into a Tournament Party for a slight extra fee of $30 for materials. Ask any JJ Team Member for more info!
