Q.  Can I rent your jumps for my
      backyard party?
Sorry, no. These are probably too big for your backyard anyway! Come to our place and we'll do all the setup and cleanup!
 Q. What are the Age and Height/Weight limits?
Our Jumps are pretty robust. They can handle adults easily, but we may have to limit the number who jump at once if there are lots of big guys!
 
 Q. What happens after I book my party online?
Within 24 hours we will contact you to make sure all the details are perfect.
Download the Jumping Jacks invitation and waiver along with instructions, and mail them to your guests. Everyone entering the Arena must have a signed waiver. Minors must have waiver signed by parents. No exceptions!
2 days before your party we'll contact you to get a final count for the party.
On the day of the party, everyone should come 10 minutes early so we can check everyone’s Liability Waiver and give a safety demonstration. 

No need to clean your house, prepare food, or entertain your guests - we'll do all that for you!
 
 Q. Can I bring in my own food?
You can absolutely bring your own cake! In addition, we’ll allow limited light snacks to be brought in. Give us a call or send us an email and let’s talk about it!
